Mongolian Ground Beef

Description

Mongolian Ground Beef is a 20 minute stir fry bursting with sweet and savory Asian flavors. It’s a perfect weeknight meal to come back to again and again.

We love our Mongolian Beef, based off of the popular takeout dish, but this time we’re simplifying it for you.

Give this easier, ground beef version a try in your kitchen tonight!

Ingredients

  • 2 tbsp oil
  • 1.5lbs Ground Beef
  • 6-7 garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 2 tbsp ginger, finely chopped
  • 6 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1/4 cup brown sugar
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp corn starch
  • 1/4 tsp black pepper
  • 2 tbsp samal oelek

Instructions

  1. Heat the oil in a skillet or wok over high heat. Add the ground beef and cook, breaking up with a spoon. Add ginger and garlic and cook until beef is browned and no more liquid remains in the pan.
  2. Combine soy sauce, brown sugar, chicken stock, corn starch sambal and pepper in a bowl. Add to the meat and combine well. Simmer a couple of minutes, until sauce thickens.
  3. Garnish with diced green onions and sesame seeds. Serve over hot rice or Asian noodles.
